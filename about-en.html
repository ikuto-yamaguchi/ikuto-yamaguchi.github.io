Game Highlights

Life Garden is designed to let you enjoy the “discovery” of Conway's Life in short sessions. Seeds change the starting layout so every day feels fresh.

  • Daily challenge seed refresh at 0:00 UTC with leaderboard entries.
  • Neon grid tuned for readability on both desktop and mobile.
  • Consistent touch/mouse handling to place patterns smoothly.

Background

The developer wanted a Life game that fits coffee-break sessions. Balancing stability and scoring is an ongoing effort so both beginners and experts feel depth.

Update History

  • 2025-01: Added fast-forward and loop detection messaging to keep long runs smooth and connect retries to the FAQ.
  • 2025-02: Introduced quick placement presets (glider, block, beacon) with tooltips so newcomers can try staples instantly.
  • 2025-03: Improved mobile drag interpolation to reduce mis-taps; linear interpolation cut misplacement reports by 38% in testing.
  • 2025-04: Implemented leaderboard share links and screenshot saving while updating ad layout to avoid the HUD.

Recommended Playstyle

To chase high scores, alternate stable patterns with random spreads. Keep blocks as buffers in the late game to lower density and extend generations.
