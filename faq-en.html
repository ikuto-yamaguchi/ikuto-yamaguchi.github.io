Q. Is it comfortable on smartphones?

Yes. UI spacing is tuned for taps and drags, keeping margin above and below the grid to avoid accidental touches.

Q. Where are ads shown?

Ads are limited to non-interactive areas such as below explanations or near the footer to avoid interrupting play. See the privacy policy for details.

Q. Is it suitable for kids?

There is no violent content. Rules are simple, and there is no online PvP, so it is safe to play solo.

Business hours: weekdays 10:00-18:00 (JST). Average response target: within 24 hours. Supported languages: Japanese/English. Inquiry logs are deleted after 90 days.
